## Five-Tier Provenance Applied To Market Intelligence

T1 VERIFIED
Real measurement. Live API data. Actual purchase records.
Example: Google Search Console showing 4,200 monthly searches verified.

T2 EXTRACTED
From a primary source with URL and date.
Example: Statista report stating market size with direct citation.

T3 INFERRED
Multiple converging signals pointing to same conclusion.
Example: Three independent sources all show rising demand for X.

T4 MODELED
Projection or estimate. Always disclosed as such.
Example: Estimated $2.4M market opportunity based on T3 signals.

T5 FOUNDATIONAL
Guiding principle that shapes interpretation.
Example: Markets with high search volume and low quality supply
represent the best opportunity for new entrants.
